Art has played a central role in my life for as long as I can remember, whether we’re talking fine arts of theatre. I owe much of this love for the arts to my inspiring art teacher at school in rural New Hampshire.

I’ve spent most of my adult life in Europe, throwing myself into the arts communities in the places I’ve lived, notably France and the Czech Republic, while pursuing another passion of mine: learning and teaching languages.

Since early 2019 I have been working for a painting company in Minnesota specializing in the restoration and preservation of historical houses. Along the way I started doing house portraits for some of our clients. This experience has enabled me to fuse my passion for drawing with my interest in architectural history.
