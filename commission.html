Get a quote on your house portrait

Email me at alex@alexgeerart.com to commission a portrait of your home. I'll need a high resolution photo of your house, so think about what angle you like it to be drawn from. If possible, try to get a photo with a good light source and minimize shadows and sun glare.

Prices vary, depending on the size of the work and the complexity of the house. The average price is $250 for a portrait with an image measuring roughly 8 x 10 inches on a paper measuring 11 x 14. This will allow room for matting before framing. Other sizes are possible, depending what you would like. Email me or phone me and we can discuss your preferences, as well as framing and shipping options. Phone: (612) 607 4784

I can also arrange prints of your portrait, or to use the image on stationery (letterheads, greeting cards) or accessories.
